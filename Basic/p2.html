There are 4 different combination in css..

  1. Descendent Selector (using SPANCE)
  2. Child Selector (USING > SYMBOL)
  3. Adjacent Sibling Selector (USING + SYMBOL)
  4. General Sibling Selector (USING ~ SYMOBOL)
